Rules
  1. All participants must be older than 18, and must bring a valid form of identification.
  2. Participants must abide by the expectations set in the MLH Code of Conduct, or you will be asked to leave the event.
  3. Teams may be comprised of up to five members.
  4. Projects should be original works created on site. Coming with an idea in mind is perfectly fine, working on an existing project is not.
  5. All submissions must be received through DevPost by 5:00PM on Sunday.
  6. Students are permitted to come and go from the venue as they please.
  7. Anything you create is your work, HackPSU and its partners have no claim over intellectual property produced at the event.
FAQ
