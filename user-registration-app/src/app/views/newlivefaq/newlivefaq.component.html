How do I submit a Project? add

All projects will be submitted through the HackPSU Devpost. Do NOT submit your project via email, Discord, slide into a DM, messenger pigeon, drone, overnight express mail, etc. Both hardware and software projects are allowed. Only one Devpost submission per team is needed. Participants/teams will be asked to upload a 2 minute video showcasing their project as well as questions to answers about the logistics and specifications of your build.
